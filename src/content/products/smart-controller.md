## Product Overview

Smart Controller is a next-generation IoT smart controller that integrates data acquisition, logic control, remote communication, and human-machine interaction capabilities. It is the ideal choice for Industry 4.0 upgrades.

## Core Advantages

### Comprehensive Connectivity
Built-in Wi-Fi, 4G, and Ethernet interfaces with support for Modbus RTU/TCP and OPC UA industrial protocols, enabling seamless integration with a wide range of devices and systems.

### Intelligent Data Analytics
Built-in data acquisition and storage capabilities with support for edge computing and cloud data synchronization, enabling real-time monitoring of equipment operating status.

### Intuitive HMI
7-inch color touchscreen with a user-friendly graphical interface, reducing the learning curve and improving operational efficiency.
