## Product Overview

Widget Pro is a high-performance industrial sensor designed for demanding industrial environments. Featuring advanced sensing technology, it delivers exceptional measurement accuracy and long-term stability.

### High-Precision Measurement
Utilizing advanced MEMS sensing technology with accuracy up to ±0.1%, meeting the stringent precision requirements of industrial automation.

### Rugged and Durable
Full stainless steel 316L housing with IP67 protection rating, ensuring stable operation in extreme temperatures from -40°C to 125°C.

### Easy Integration
Standard industrial interfaces (4-20mA / 0-10V) with support for multiple process connection types, enabling rapid integration into existing systems.
