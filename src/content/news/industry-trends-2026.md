As the global economy continues to develop, the B2B industry is undergoing profound transformation. Here are five key trends to watch in 2026.

## 1. Accelerated Digital Transformation

In the post-pandemic era, the pace of digital transformation among B2B enterprises has further accelerated. From online inquiries to remote technical support, digital tools are reshaping traditional business models.

**Key Insight**: By the end of 2026, an estimated 75% of B2B procurement processes are expected to be fully digitized.

## 2. Sustainability as a Core Competitive Advantage

An increasing number of customers are incorporating suppliers' ESG performance into their procurement decisions. Green manufacturing, carbon footprint tracking, and the circular economy are no longer optional — they are essential.

## 3. Supply Chain Resilience Optimization

With persistent uncertainty in global supply chains, enterprises are shifting from an "efficiency-first" to a "resilience-first" approach, building diversified and localized supply chain systems.

## 4. Deep Integration of AI and IoT

The convergence of artificial intelligence and IoT technologies is transforming the manufacturing landscape. Predictive maintenance, intelligent quality inspection, and automated production are becoming industry standards.

## 5. Parallel Localization and Globalization

While expanding globally, localized service capabilities have become increasingly important. Enterprises that can provide rapid response and localized support will gain a competitive edge.
